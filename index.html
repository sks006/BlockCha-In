  • Quantum Resistant Ledger

    RL has quantum resistance as its core focus. It utilizes the IETF-specified XMSS (eXtended Merkle Signature Scheme), which is a hash-based, forward-secure signature scheme. XMSS ensures minimal security assumptions and reusable addresses, and it has received NIST approval.

  • Mochimo

    Mochimo is a lesser-known cryptocurrency that emphasizes quantum resistance. It uses a custom signature scheme called XMSS-MT (XMSS with Merkle Tree).

  • IOTA

    IOTA is a unique cryptocurrency that uses a Tangle (directed acyclic graph) instead of a traditional blockchain. It claims to be quantum-resistant due to its Winternitz-based signature scheme.
